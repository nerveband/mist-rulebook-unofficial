# MIST Quiz Bowl Topic 5:
## Current Events

For MIST Quiz Bowl Topic 5: Current Events, the materials provide a broad snapshot
of major global developments through timelines and summaries of significant events
and figures from the year. This year, the first part of all Round 1 questions will
highlight information not explicitly stated in the provided materials, encouraging
students to stay informed by following news beyond the assigned readings. This
approach emphasizes the importance of context, awareness, and staying engaged with
current events worldwide.

Questions will be asked from January 1, 2025 until December 31, 2025.
