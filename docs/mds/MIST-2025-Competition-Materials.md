# MIST 2025 Competitions Rulebook & Materials

> Source: [https://www.getmistified.com/news/2024/11/20/2025-competitions-rulebook](https://www.getmistified.com/news/2024/11/20/2025-competitions-rulebook)
> Last Updated: December 9, 2025

---

## Overview

The Muslim Interscholastic Tournament released updated competition rules for 2025/2026 with major revisions reflecting "extensive collaboration and regional feedback" to improve clarity and participant experience.

---

## Key Rule Changes

### Knowledge Test
- All questions standardized to multiple choice or "select all that apply" formats

### Qur'an Memorization
- Surah Ya-Seen repositioned to Level 3
- Physical mushaf no longer required
- Updated rubric includes tie-breaker considerations

### Extemporaneous Speaking
- Preparation time increased to 30 minutes

### Arts Competitions
- Pre-interview scoring capped at 80 points; only top 15 competitors can exceed this threshold

### Original Oratory & Spoken Word
- Memorization now officially scored within rubric

### Debate
- Revised speech times and rebuttal order
- New standardized tie-breaker criteria for preliminary rounds
- Updated ballot structure

### Math Olympics
- Expanded content coverage with supplementary practice materials

### MIST Bowl
- Round 1 focused exclusively on current events with revised toss-up structure
- Adjusted points in Rounds 2-3 for better balance
- Topics reduced from six to five

### Humanitarian Service
- Partnership shifted from IRUSA to HHRD
- New community engagement component added
