This is a **pilot competition specific to MIST Nashville**. These rules may differ from other regions.

## Overview

The Soccer competition offers a great team-building activity in the form of a fun, athletic competition. This competition is organized in a **bracket format**, and winning teams advance to the next round.

<Aside type="note" title="Scoring Note">
This competition **does not count towards points**; however, awards will be presented to the winning teams.
</Aside>

<Aside type="caution" title="Important Requirement">
Competitors must compete in at least **one competition from another category** in order to qualify as a Soccer competitor. Failure to participate in MIST competitions will result in **immediate disqualification**.
</Aside>

## Registration Guidelines

1. Each team may register a **maximum of fourteen (14)** members
2. A **minimum of six (6)** members required to form a team
3. Schools that cannot register 6 players may **collaborate with another school** (with approval)

## Competition Format

- **Separate competitions** for brothers and sisters
- Each school may bring both a sisters' soccer team and a brothers' soccer team
- Bracket-style tournament with advancement

## Game Rules

All standard soccer rules apply, with the following **MIST-specific modifications**:

<Steps>

1. All MIST rules and regulations, including **dress code**, apply

2. Failure to participate in MIST competitions results in immediate **disqualification**

3. Teams must wear **matching colors** (matching socks, shorts, and jerseys)

4. All players **MUST wear shin guards**

5. Players are **NOT allowed** to wear cleats with metal studs

6. Shorts **MUST reach the knees** - no short shorts allowed

7. Each team consists of **6 players on the field** (no goalie), with a 3x2 goal

8. **No goal tending** - players cannot stand directly in front of goal for extended periods
   - Players seen goal tending will be asked to step away
   - After multiple warnings: possible ejection (referee's discretion)

9. Players who **purposely foul** other players will be immediately ejected

10. **No stoppage time** added in any rounds

11. **Ties in preliminary round** are counted as ties (no extra time or penalties)
    - In playoffs: 5-minute extra period, then penalties on regular-sized goals if still tied

12. **Playoff advancement** determined by points:
    - Win = 3 points
    - Tie = 1 point each
    - Loss = 0 points

13. **Unlimited substitutions** at the center line at any time
    - Player coming off must exit first before new player enters
    - Standard soccer substitution rules apply

14. All free kicks are **two touch (indirect)**
    - Any goal from a direct free kick will be waved off

15. **No throw-ins** - if ball goes out of bounds, opposite team plays in with a ground pass

16. **10-goal mercy rule** in the second half

17. **Unsportsmanlike conduct** will not be tolerated (see below)

18. **Gender separation enforced** - brothers cannot attend sisters' games and vice versa

</Steps>

## Point System (Preliminary Rounds)

| Result | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Win | 3 points |
| Tie | 1 point |
| Loss | 0 points |

## Unsportsmanlike Conduct

The following offenses will result in a **yellow card or red card**:

| Offense | Penalty |
|---------|---------|
| Cursing at supervisor, official, or opposing player | Yellow/Red Card |
| Insulting supervisor, official, or opposing player | Yellow/Red Card |
| Cursing out of anger/impulse (excessive) | Yellow Card (referee's discretion) |
| Participating in a fight | **AUTOMATIC EJECTION** |

<Aside type="danger" title="Gender Separation">
No brother (whether audience or player) is allowed to attend a sister's game, and vice versa.
</Aside>

## Uniform Requirements

**All players must have:**
- [ ] Matching jerseys
- [ ] Matching shorts (must reach knees)
- [ ] Matching socks
- [ ] Shin guards (mandatory)
- [ ] Appropriate cleats (no metal studs)

## Key Differences from Standard Soccer

| Standard Rule | MIST Nashville Rule |
|---------------|---------------------|
| 11 players with goalie | 6 players, no goalie |
| Throw-ins | Ground passes |
| Direct free kicks | All free kicks are indirect |
| Standard game length | 6-minute halves |
| Standard goal | 3x2 goal |
