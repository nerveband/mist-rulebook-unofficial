<Aside type="caution" title="Region-Specific Rules">
The rules on this page are **specific to MIST Nashville** and may differ from the national rulebook. These are pilot competitions being tested at the regional level.
</Aside>

## Pilot Competitions

MIST Nashville offers the following pilot sports competitions in addition to the standard MIST competitions:

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Volleyball" icon="star">
    Team sport with 5-8 players per team. Best of three games format with rally scoring to 15 points.

    [View Volleyball Rules →](/regions/nashville/volleyball/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Soccer" icon="star">
    Team sport with 6-14 players per team. 6v6 format with no goalie. Awards presented but no points towards overall score.

    [View Soccer Rules →](/regions/nashville/soccer/)
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Important Notes

- All pilot competition competitors **must also be registered for a scholastic competition**
- These competitions follow standard MIST rules and dress code requirements
- Schools may form **coalitions** with other schools if they cannot meet minimum team requirements
- Contact MIST Nashville for any questions about these pilot competitions
