## Study Recommendations

1. **Read actively** - Take notes, highlight key passages, and write summaries
2. **Discuss with teammates** - Form study groups to discuss themes and concepts
3. **Connect the dots** - Look for connections between the books and current events
4. **Review key facts** - Pay attention to names, dates, and specific details that may appear on tests
5. **Understand themes** - Go beyond memorization to understand the broader arguments and themes
