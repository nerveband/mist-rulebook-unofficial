Everything you need to know as a MIST competitor.

## Eligibility

- Must be a **high school student** with current and active enrollment
- Must compete with your enrolled school's team
- Only **one team per school** is allowed
- May only compete in **one competition per category**
- Students in sports **must also register for a non-sport competition**

Submit these via MyMIST (see [Early Submissions](/rulebook/early-submissions/)):
- [Prepared Essay](/rulebook/writing-oratory/prepared-essay/)
- [Short Fiction](/rulebook/writing-oratory/short-fiction/)
- [Poetry](/rulebook/writing-oratory/poetry/)
- [Short Film](/rulebook/group-projects/short-film/)

- [Science Fair](/rulebook/group-projects/science-fair/) Abstract
- [Social Media](/rulebook/group-projects/social-media/)
- [Humanitarian Service](/rulebook/group-projects/humanitarian-service/)
- [Digital Art](/rulebook/arts/digital-art/)

### Late Submission Penalties
- Within 24 hours: **-5 points**
- Each additional day: **-10 points**
- Beyond 3 days: **Disqualified**

## What to Bring

### Tournament Day Essentials
- [ ] MIST name badge (wear at all times)
- [ ] Competition schedule
- [ ] 3 hard copies of early submissions (except Poetry, Prepared Essay, Short Fiction)
- [ ] Any required materials for your competitions

### Competition-Specific Items
- **[Knowledge Tests](/rulebook/knowledge-quran/knowledge-tests/)**: Your own copy of the [assigned book](/knowledge-test-books/) (no annotations!)
- **[Quran Recitation](/rulebook/knowledge-quran/quran-recitation/)**: Your own Quran (no annotations or color-coded versions)
- **[2D](/rulebook/arts/2d-art/)/[3D Art](/rulebook/arts/3d-art/)**: Completed artwork, drafts, written statement
- **[Fashion Design](/rulebook/arts/fashion-design/)**: Portfolio with all required elements
- **[Oratory](/rulebook/writing-oratory/original-oratory/)/[Spoken Word](/rulebook/writing-oratory/spoken-word/)**: 3 copies of your piece
- **[Science Fair](/rulebook/group-projects/science-fair/)**: Display board, abstract copies, lab notebook

## Top 10 Rules Competitors Break

1. **Putting your name on submissions** - Use MIST ID only!
2. **Missing early submission deadlines** - Results in point deductions
3. **Not bringing hard copies** - Many competitions require 3 copies
4. **Using AI** - Grounds for disqualification in all competitions
5. **Annotating books/Qurans** - No highlighting, post-its, or margin notes
6. **Going overtime** - Results in being ranked last
7. **Not attending registered competitions** - 5-point deduction per competition + sports disqualification
8. **Leaving workshops early** - Must stay until the end for spirit points
9. **Leaving tournament early without permission** - Request 7+ days in advance
10. **Not checking for updates** - Failure to read communications is on you

## Submission Format Guidelines

All written submissions should be:
- Typed and double-spaced
- Size 12 font, Times New Roman
- PDF format
- Cover page with title, MIST ID, and competition name
- **No name or identifying markers**
- File named: `MISTID_COMPETITIONNAME`

## Day-of Checklist

### Before Arriving
- [ ] Confirm your competition times
- [ ] Pack all required materials
- [ ] Review rules for your competitions
- [ ] Get a good night's sleep!

### At the Tournament
- [ ] Arrive on time for registration
- [ ] Get your MIST badge and wear it
- [ ] Check the schedule for any changes
- [ ] Be at competitions **early**
- [ ] Attend your registered workshops
- [ ] Stay until the tournament is over

## Tips for Success

1. **Read the rules** - Know exactly what's expected
2. **Practice** - Rehearse presentations and performances
3. **Prepare early** - Don't wait until the last minute
4. **Ask questions** - If you don't know, ASK!
5. **Support your team** - Attend others' competitions when allowed
6. **Have fun** - Make connections and enjoy the experience

## Getting Help

- **During tournament**: Find a MIST Representative
- **Before tournament**: Contact your Regional Headquarters
- **Lost badge**: See a MIST Representative immediately
