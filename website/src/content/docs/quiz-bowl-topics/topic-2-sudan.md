## Sudan Beyond the Headlines

This topic invites students to engage with a mix of short articles highlighting major headlines alongside longer readings and personal reflections that provide essential context. Together, these sources help competitors move beyond breaking-news narratives to better understand Sudan's history, humanitarian challenges, and the lived experiences of its people.

**Goal:** Emphasize our roles as informed global citizens and the responsibility we have to engage thoughtfully with global challenges.

## Source Materials

Questions will be asked from the following:

1. [**Sudan**](https://www.britannica.com/place/Sudan) - Britannica
2. [**Sudan: A War of Atrocities**](https://www.ohchr.org/sites/default/files/documents/hrbodies/hrcouncil/sessions-regular/session60/advance-version/a-hrc-60-22-aev.pdf) - Report of The Independent International Fact-Finding Mission for The Sudan (UNHRC) (PDF)
3. [**A Simple Guide to What Is Happening in Sudan**](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cjel2nn22z9o) - BBC
4. [**What Is the Extent of Sudan's Humanitarian Crisis?**](https://www.cfr.org/in-brief/what-extent-sudans-humanitarian-crisis) - Council on Foreign Relations (CFR)
5. [**Humanitarian Crisis: The Sudan Story**](https://globalhealthsciences.ucsf.edu/humanitarian-crisis-the-sudan-story/) - UCSF Global Health Sciences (Blog)
6. [**Humanitarian Crisis: The Sudan Story**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wYLKC6M3hzQ) - UCSF (YouTube Video)

## What's Fair Game

All information in the materials is testable, including:
- Graph/figure content (labels, numbers, statistics)
- Dates
- Numbers and statistics
- Names and backgrounds of speakers or individuals mentioned
- Definitions for key terms

:::note
References within the materials do not need to be memorized.
:::

## Key Themes to Study

- Sudan's geography and regional significance
- Historical context (independence, Bashir regime, Darfur)
- Current conflict between SAF and RSF
- Key figures: General al-Burhan, Hemedti
- Humanitarian crisis and displacement
- International response and aid challenges
