## The Flow of Water: Balancing Scarcity, Use, and Waste

This topic explores how human systems like households, agriculture, industry, and public infrastructure interact with water resources, and what happens when access and usage is imbalanced. Using data, reports, and real-world examples, these materials highlight patterns of shortage, overconsumption, and inefficiency around the world.

**Goal:** Consider how decisions surrounding water use affect health, food security, and long-term sustainability across communities.

## Source Materials

Questions will be asked from the following:

1. **How AI Uses Our Drinking Water** - BBC World Service
2. **Water Scarcity** - World Wildlife Fund (WWF)
3. **Drinking Water** - World Health Organization (WHO)
4. **Water Scarcity in Agriculture: The Greatest Threat to Global Food Security** - University of Tennessee
5. **The United Nations World Water Development Report 2024: Water for Prosperity and Peace**
