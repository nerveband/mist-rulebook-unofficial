If shooting hoops and hearing your sneakers squeak on the court is your thing, basketball is the perfect sport to get your blood pumping and adrenaline rushing.

## Format
In-Person

<Aside type="caution" title="Important Requirement">
Any competitor who registers for a sport **must also be registered for a scholastic competition**. If competitors partake in a sport but do not attend their other registered competition, they will be **disqualified** from their sport. This means teams risk losing their rankings for sports they may have already won.
</Aside>

## Registration Guidelines

1. A minimum of **five (5)** and a maximum of **ten (10)** competitors can register per school.
   - Separate registration for Brothers' Basketball and Sisters' Basketball

2. Only **one (1) Brothers' team** and **one (1) Sisters' team** allowed per school.

3. Your school may **collaborate with another school** to form a coalition.
   - All points and awards shared equally between collaborating schools
   - Check with your Regional Headquarters for more information

4. If a student does not attend their academic competition, they are **disqualified** from basketball and cannot register for nationals.

## Game Rules

MIST follows **standard high school basketball rules** (offense: traveling, double dribbling, charging; defense: goaltending, blocking, pushing).

<Steps>

1. Students must be enrolled in **another competition** in addition to sports.

2. All MIST rules and regulations, including **dress code**, also apply.

3. **Failure to participate** in MIST competitions and/or workshops results in immediate disqualification.

4. Game consists of **two 15-minute halves**.
   - If tied after regulation (except playoffs), recorded as tie game
   - No overtime for regular games

5. Playoff games: If tied after regulation, **5-minute overtime periods** until winner determined.

6. Clock runs **non-stop until final 2 minutes** of each half.
   - During last 2 minutes, clock stops on all whistles
   - Official/team time-out stops clock during first 18 minutes

7. Each team may need **one person at scorer's table** to operate clock and keep score.
   - Forfeit may result if team doesn't comply

8. Teams **MUST have matching attire**:
   - Jersey with numbers (0-99)
   - Same colored shirts
   - **No "Shirts vs Skins"** or taped-on numbers

9. **No basket scored** on player control (offensive) foul, regardless of when ball is released.

10. **Time-outs:** 1 minute each, **3 per game**
    - Half-time: 2 minutes

11. **Two technical fouls** on a player or coach = automatic ejection
    - Ejected player must leave playing area

12. Only **one coach and one assistant coach** per team on bench.

13. **Bonus free throws:**
    - 7th team foul: one-and-one
    - 10th team foul: two free throws
    - 3-point attempt foul (no basket): three free throws

14. **35-point mercy rule** after 10 minutes in second half.
    - If team drags or allows catch-up, game stopped immediately

15. **Substitutes** must be reported to score table and beckoned in by officials.
    - Illegal substitution = technical foul

16. **Backboard slapping:** Not inherently a technical foul, but intentionally hitting hard enough to alter ball flight = technical foul

17. **Unsportsmanlike conduct** will NOT be tolerated.

18. **Technical foul behaviors** (possible game suspension):
    - Cursing at officials, supervisors, or opposing players
    - Insulting officials, supervisors, or opposing players
    - **Fighting = immediate disqualification**
    - Other unsportsmanlike acts

19. All unsportsmanlike fouls: **two free throws + possession**, regardless of player or bench foul.

20. **Top three regional teams** eligible to compete at National tournament.

21. All competitors must have **registered and competed at Regional** to be eligible for Nationals.

</Steps>

## Game Format Summary

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Halves | Two 15-minute halves |
| Half-time | 2 minutes |
| Time-outs | 3 per game (1 minute each) |
| Overtime | 5-minute periods (playoffs only) |
| Mercy Rule | 35 points after 10 min in 2nd half |
| Bonus | 7th foul: 1-and-1 / 10th foul: 2 FTs |

## Foul Summary

| Foul Count | Result |
|------------|--------|
| 7th team foul | One-and-one free throws |
| 10th team foul | Two free throws |
| 2 technical fouls (player/coach) | Automatic ejection |
| Unsportsmanlike foul | 2 free throws + possession |
| Fighting | Immediate disqualification |

## Uniform Requirements

Teams MUST match through their attire:
- [ ] Jerseys with numbers (0-99)
- [ ] OR Same colored shirts
- [ ] Numbers must be visible (no taped numbers)
- [ ] "Shirts vs Skins" NOT allowed

<Aside type="note" title="Coalition Teams">
Schools may collaborate with another school to compete together. All points and awards are shared equally between collaborating schools. Check with your Regional HQ for details.
</Aside>

## National Tournament Eligibility

- Top **three (3)** regional teams eligible for Nationals
- Must have **registered AND competed** at Regional tournament
- Must have **attended scholastic competition** to remain eligible
