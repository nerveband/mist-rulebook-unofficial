Compete in basketball while representing your school and demonstrating good sportsmanship.

**Format:** In-Person  
**Gender Segregated:** Yes

## Registration Guidelines

1. Teams must follow size requirements set by Regional HQ.
2. This is a **team competition**.
3. Competition is **gender segregated**:
   - Brothers Basketball
   - Sisters Basketball

:::caution[Important]
Students registered in a sport **MUST** register for another non-sport competition. Failure to attend a registered competition will result in disqualification from sports and point deductions.
:::

## General Rules

1. Standard basketball rules apply with MIST modifications.

2. Game length and format determined by Regional HQ.

3. All players must wear appropriate athletic attire.

4. Good sportsmanship is required at all times.

5. Technical fouls for unsportsmanlike conduct will result in penalties.

## Team Requirements

- Minimum and maximum roster sizes set by Regional HQ
- All players must be registered MIST participants
- Substitutions allowed per standard rules

## Tournament Format

Format varies by region but typically includes:
- Pool play or round robin
- Single or double elimination brackets
- Championship game

## Sportsmanship

MIST emphasizes good sportsmanship:
- Respect referees and their decisions
- Respect opposing players
- No taunting or trash talk
- Shake hands before and after games
- Represent your school and community well

## Equipment

- Basketball provided by MIST
- Players should bring appropriate footwear
- Uniforms or matching team shirts recommended

For specific rules regarding:
- Team size

- Tournament format
- Registration deadlines
- Venue information
