Express your creativity through fashion design by creating a cohesive collection that reflects this year's theme.

## Format
In-Person

## Submission Requirements

**On-Site:**
- Collection Portfolio

## Registration Guidelines

1. No more than **four (4) students** from each school can register.
2. This competition is **NOT** a team effort. Each competitor must turn in separate work.
3. MIST ID and order of competition will be pre-assigned randomly.

## Portfolio Requirements

Your portfolio must include:

<Steps>

1. **Cover/Presentation Case** — Be creative!

2. **Philosophy Statement** (500 words max)
   - How you'll use the theme as inspiration for your collection
   - Example: "Loyalty + Environment (trees, landscape)"
   - Serves as your written component

3. **Mood/Inspiration Page** — Images that inspire your collection

4. **Fabric Swatch/Color Palette Page**

5. **Six (6) Outfits** in one of these combinations:
   - OPTION 1: 3 adult female + 3 adult male outfits
   - OPTION 2: 6 complete male OR 6 complete female outfits

</Steps>

## Outfit Requirements

For each of the 6 outfits, submit:

| Item | Specifications |
|------|----------------|
| **Illustration** | 5"×7" to 8.5"×11", in color, matted/mounted, in page protector |
| **Flat Sketches** | 2 per outfit (front & back), black and white, 12 total |
| **Description** | Sewing notes and hard-to-see details |

### Illustration Guidelines
- Must be hand-drawn (no photographs)
- Clean and neatly mounted/matted
- Acceptable media: colored pencils, charcoal, pastels, watercolor, colored pen/ink, crayons
- Outfits can be on a figure or as colored flats

<Aside type="tip" title="Bonus Points">
Extra points available for accessories: shoes, pins, jewelry, etc. (Outfits without shoes will NOT be disqualified)
</Aside>

## Design Requirements

<Steps>

1. All designs labeled with **MIST ID ONLY**.

2. Provide a **common feature** within the set (similar buttons, colors, etc.).

3. Designs must be **modest** while exuding creativity.

4. Designs should inspire **comfort, confidence, and dignity**.

5. Be **original** in all designs.

</Steps>

## Interview Process

- Each participant interviewed for **5 minutes**
- If more than 15 entries, only top 15 (based on portfolio) will be interviewed
- Interview questions found in Fashion Design Ballot

### Interview Topics
- Inspiration behind the design
- What you like most and dislike most
- Outstanding or unusual details
- What makes your designs different from others in the market

## Checklist

- [ ] Fashion Design Portfolio with philosophy
- [ ] 6 outfit illustrations (color, mounted)
- [ ] 12 flat sketches (black and white)
- [ ] Mood/inspiration page
- [ ] Fabric swatch/color palette page
- [ ] Labeled with MIST ID and competition name
