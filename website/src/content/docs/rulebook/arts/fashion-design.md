## On-Site Submission Requirements
- Collection Portfolio

## Registration Guidelines

1. No more than **four (4) students** from each school can register for this competition.
2. This competition is **NOT** a team effort. Each competitor must turn in a separate piece of work.
3. MIST ID and order of competition will be pre-assigned on a random selection basis.

## Portfolio Requirements

1. All designs must be labeled with **MIST ID ONLY**. Competitor name must not appear anywhere.

2. Your portfolio should have a **cover or presentation case**. Be creative!

3. **Philosophy** (500 words max): Explain how you use your inspiration to make a collection.
   - Your inspiration is this year's theme and can be combined with other inspirations.
   - Example: Theme + Environment (trees, landscape, etc.)

4. **Mood/Inspiration page**: Images that inspire your collection, relating to your philosophy.

5. **Fabric swatch/color palette page**

6. **Six (6) outfits** in one of these combinations:
   - OPTION 1: 3 complete adult female outfits AND 3 complete adult male outfits
   - OPTION 2: 6 complete male outfits OR 6 complete female outfits

## For Each Outfit

Each of your 6 outfits must include:

1. **One illustration** (5"x7" to 8.5"x11") of each design as worn, in color, matted and mounted on heavy paper or poster board, enclosed in a page protector.

2. **Two flat design sketches** (front and back) for each outfit - total of 12 flat sketches in black and white.

3. **Description and sewing notes** including details that are hard to see in your design.

4. The illustration must be clean, neatly mounted or matted. Acceptable media: colored pencils, charcoal, pastels, watercolor, colored pen/ink, or crayons.

5. **Extra design items** (shoes, accessories, jewelry) can earn extra points. Outfits without shoes will NOT be disqualified.

## Design Requirements

- Competitors must provide a **common feature** within a set (similar buttons, colors, etc.)
- Designs must be **modest** in nature while exuding creativity
- Designs should inspire comfort, confidence, and dignity
- Competitors are encouraged to be **original** in all designs

## Interview Process

1. Each participant will be interviewed for **five (5) minutes**.

2. Judges will ask about:
   - Inspiration behind the design
   - What you like most and dislike most
   - Details, parts, and characteristics that are outstanding or unusual
   - What makes your designs different from others in the market

3. If more than **15 entries**, only top 15 (based on portfolio) will be interviewed.

## Scoring Rubric

| Section | Category | Points |
|---------|----------|--------|
| A | Technical | /5 |
| B | Purpose | /10 |
| B | Skills and Technique | /20 |
| C | Aesthetic and Design | /20 |
| C | Creativity | /20 |
| D | Application of Theme | /25 |
| **Total** | | **/100** |

### Section A: Technical (5 points)
- Participant is on time and dressed for interview
- Submission meets formatting guidelines
- Portfolio includes six (6) full outfits
- Philosophy is 500 words or less

### Section B: Production Quality (30 points)
**Overall:**
- Common theme/feature across the collection
- Portfolio is well organized, neat, and professional
- Description and sewing notes are thorough

**Skill and Technique:**
- Effective choice and control of media and tools
- Superior level of skills in production and execution
- Portfolio required time and patience

### Section C: Presentation Quality (40 points)
**Aesthetics and Design:**
- Interesting seams and shapes in clothing
- Selected fabrication (colors, prints, trims) enhances designs while remaining cohesive
- Designs inspire comfort, confidence, and dignity
- Designs appear possible to construct/are functional

**Creativity:**
- Expression is unique and imaginative
- Designs are creative while maintaining modest nature

### Section D: Application of Theme (25 points)
- Strong, clearly evident relevance to theme
- Theme expressed in unique or outstanding way
- Purpose and inspiration clearly articulated
