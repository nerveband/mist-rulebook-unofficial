## Registration Guidelines

1. **Team of 3-5 students** from each school.
2. This is a **team competition**.

## Early Submission Requirements

Submit via MyMIST **two weeks before** the Regional Tournament:
- Business Venture Proposal document

## What to Include in Your Proposal

1. **Executive Summary**
   - Brief overview of business concept
   - Mission statement

2. **Problem/Opportunity**
   - What need does your business address?
   - Market research and analysis

3. **Solution/Product**
   - Description of product or service
   - How it solves the identified problem

4. **Target Market**
   - Who are your customers?
   - Market size and demographics

5. **Business Model**
   - How will you make money?
   - Pricing strategy

6. **Marketing Strategy**
   - How will you reach customers?
   - Branding and promotion plans

7. **Financial Projections**
   - Startup costs
   - Revenue projections
   - Break-even analysis

8. **Team**
   - Roles and responsibilities
   - Relevant skills and experience

9. **Islamic Integration**
   - How does your business incorporate Islamic values?
   - Ethical considerations

## On-Site Presentation

1. Teams will present their business plan to judges.
2. Presentation time limit will be specified by Regional HQ.
3. Be prepared for Q&A from judges.
4. Visual aids (slides, prototypes) are encouraged.

## Judging Criteria

- Viability of business concept
- Quality of market research
- Financial soundness
- Creativity and innovation
- Integration of Islamic values
- Presentation quality
- Team collaboration
