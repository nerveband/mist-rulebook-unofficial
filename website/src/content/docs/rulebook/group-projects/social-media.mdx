Take your online social habits to the next level by creating a social media presence for your MIST team! Showcase your content strategy and digital marketing skills.

## Format
In-Person

## Submission Requirements

**Early Submission (2 weeks prior):**
- Link to social media platform
- Typed statement submitted to MyMIST

**On-Site:**
- Three (3) copies of the typed statement

## Registration Guidelines

1. A minimum of **one (1)** and a maximum of **six (6)** people can register.
2. **One submission per school** - if multiple students register from the same school, they must work as a team.

## Procedure

<Steps>

1. MIST ID and order of competition will be **pre-assigned randomly**.

2. Report at designated time and place. Introduced to judges by **MIST ID only**.

3. Team name may appear on your social profiles.

4. Create an **Instagram or TikTok page** for your MSA or MIST team:
   - Include posts related to MIST theme (**5 posts minimum**)
   - Posts can be text, images, video, etc.
   - **Two posts** must be regarding current events, with **one being a pure news story**

5. Update platform based on your assessment of **appropriate publishing frequency** for chosen platform.
   - Updates can be text, images, video, etc.

6. Encouraged to use **infographics and editing software** for content creation.

7. Posts must be posted **before submission date**, not day of/night before.

8. If Instagram stories are posted, they must be compiled into a **highlight** on the page.

9. Be **cognizant of music/sounds** used:
   - No vulgarity, profanity, or inappropriate language
   - Contact Regional HQ if uncertain about sound/audio

10. Submit (written statement and social media link) **two (2) weeks prior** to tournament.
    - All submissions viewed on computer unless stated otherwise.

11. Typed explanation **(1000 words max, double-spaced, 12-point font, max 3 pages)** must include:
    - Social platform's content details
    - Inspiration for choosing the platform
    - Content strategy employed
    - How each team member contributed
    - Any other pertinent information

12. Interview will be **no longer than 5 minutes**.

13. Social media platform **must include**:
    - Information about the MIST team
    - Prayer times
    - Names and contact information
    - Information about MIST meetings/events
    - Link to MIST National website (www.getmistified.com) in bio/linktree
    - Link to your MIST Regional's website
    - Meeting time/place for next MIST meeting
    - Information on how to join the MIST team
    - Team's Mission statement
    - At least one Islamic article that adolescents can relate to
    - Additional features (video clips, search facility, animation, etc.)

</Steps>

<Aside type="caution" title="Content Guidelines">
Sounds must not contain vulgarity, music with profanity, or inappropriate language. If uncertain about a sound/audio, reach out to your Regional HQ for clarification.
</Aside>

## Interview Questions

Judges will ask:
1. How did you aim to convey this year's theme in your work? In what ways does your submission represent your team's point of view, imagination, creativity, and individuality?
2. Explain your purpose in developing this artwork and describe how you achieved that goal. Describe any influences from the online world that contributed to the development of your submission.
3. What elements or principles of social media did you use, and how did you select and organize the visual aspects of this work?

## Checklist

- [ ] Instagram or TikTok page with 5+ theme-related posts
- [ ] 2 current events posts (1 pure news story)
- [ ] All required page elements included
- [ ] Written statement (3 pages/1000 words max)
- [ ] Platform link submitted to MyMIST
- [ ] Submission labeled with MIST IDs and Competition Name
