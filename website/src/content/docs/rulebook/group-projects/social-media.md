Create and execute a social media campaign that spreads positive messages related to the annual MIST theme.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Format** | In-Person |
| **Type** | Team (2-4) |
| **Early Submission** | 2 weeks prior |

## Registration Guidelines

1. **Team of 2-4 students** from each school.
2. This is a **team competition**.

## Early Submission Requirements

Submit via MyMIST **two weeks before** the Regional Tournament:
- Social Media campaign documentation

## Campaign Requirements

Your campaign should include:

1. **Platform Selection**
   - Choose 1-3 social media platforms
   - Justify your platform choices

2. **Campaign Strategy**
   - Goals and objectives
   - Target audience
   - Content calendar/schedule
   - Hashtag strategy

3. **Content Creation**
   - Original graphics/images
   - Video content (if applicable)
   - Written posts/captions
   - Stories/reels content

4. **Theme Integration**
   - Clear connection to annual MIST theme
   - Islamic values incorporated

5. **Engagement Metrics**
   - Track likes, shares, comments
   - Follower growth (if applicable)
   - Reach and impressions

## Content Guidelines

- All content must be appropriate
- No copyrighted material without permission
- Must follow platform community guidelines
- Should be positive and uplifting
- Avoid controversial political content

## Submission Materials

Include in your submission:
- Screenshots of all posts
- Analytics/metrics report
- Campaign strategy document
- Written reflection on campaign

## On-Site Presentation

1. Present your campaign to judges.
2. Show examples of your content.
3. Share metrics and results.
4. Be prepared for Q&A.

## Judging Criteria

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Strategy** | Quality of planning and execution |
| **Content Quality** | Visual appeal and messaging |
| **Theme Integration** | Connection to annual theme |
| **Engagement** | Metrics and audience response |
| **Creativity** | Originality and innovation |
| **Presentation** | Quality of on-site presentation |

## Tips for Success

1. **Know your audience** - Tailor content appropriately
2. **Consistency matters** - Regular posting schedule
3. **Quality over quantity** - Better to have fewer great posts
4. **Engage with followers** - Respond to comments
5. **Track everything** - Document your metrics
