| Competition | Team Size | Early Submission |
|-------------|-----------|------------------|
| Business Venture | 1-6 | **Yes** - 2 weeks prior |
| Humanitarian Service | 1-6 | **Yes** - 2 weeks prior |
| Nasheed/Rap | 1-6 | No |
| Science Fair | 1-6 | **Yes** - 2 weeks prior |
| Short Film | 1-6 | **Yes** - 2 weeks prior |
| Social Media | 1-6 | **Yes** - 2 weeks prior |

:::caution[Early Submissions Required]
5 of 6 Group Project competitions require early submission via MyMIST Portal. Don't miss the deadline! See [Early Submissions](/rulebook/early-submissions/) for full requirements.
:::
