Pitch your innovative business idea to a panel of judges. Create a comprehensive business plan that reflects this year's theme and demonstrate your entrepreneurial vision.

## Format
In-Person

## Submission Requirements

**Early Submission (2 weeks prior):**
- Business Venture outline submitted to MyMIST

**On-Site:**
- Three (3) copies of the typed Business Venture outline

## Registration Guidelines

1. A minimum of **one (1)** and a maximum of **six (6)** people can register.
2. **One submission per MIST team/school.**

## Procedure

<Steps>

1. Each team must prepare a Business Venture project that **reflects this year's theme**.
   - A venture related to the theme will be awarded additional bonus marks.
   - No points deducted if theme is not reflected.

2. Competition consists of:
   - **Written Business Venture outline**
   - **5-7 minute presentation**

3. Each team must have a **student leader** as representative, but all team members should be involved.

4. Submit a proposal **two (2) weeks prior** to the MIST tournament on MyMIST.

5. Submit **three (3) copies** of typed Business Venture outline on the first day of tournament.

6. Present to a panel of judges for **5-7 minutes**.
   - Followed by a **2-minute Q&A session**.
   - Focus: Pitch your idea with the goal of convincing judges to invest in your venture.

7. Presentation should include **supplemental materials**:
   - Product samples
   - Pictures or visual/video aids (PowerPoint, TV advertisement, etc.)
   - Poster boards or display boards

8. Project ideas must be **original and innovative**.
   - Proof of origination should be available if requested.
   - Business idea may be new or already implemented by the presenting group.

9. Judging criteria based on official MIST Business Venture ballot.

10. Primary criteria: **"Would I invest in this company?"**

</Steps>

## Outline Requirements

The Business Venture Outline must be:
- **Typed, double-spaced, Times New Roman 12 point font**
- **Cover page** with title and MIST IDs only, stapled on left side
- Include **visual aids** (pictures, charts, graphs) - no physical samples
- **Maximum 6 pages**

### Required Sections

| Section | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Executive Summary** | Summary of venture, description, market analysis, marketing plan, and financial plan (max 2 pages) |
| **Introduction** | Describe product/service in detail - how it works, makes money, value in industry |
| **Market Analysis** | Target customers, competitors, market characteristics and size, demand |
| **Marketing Plan** | Strategy, competitive advantages, testing approach, timeline, specific strategies |
| **Financial Plan** | Budget, startup funding needs, profit potential, key financial considerations |
| **Project Viability** | Biggest threats, stability vs. risk assessment, risk factors and mitigation |
| **Discussion** | Contribution to Muslim and general community, relation to theme, expansion plans |

<Aside type="tip" title="Note">
The questions in each section are guides. Teams don't need to answer all questions, but all topics must be addressed.
</Aside>

## Checklist

- [ ] Business Venture Outline (6 pages max)
- [ ] Supplemental materials for group presentation
