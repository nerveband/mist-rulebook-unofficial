Create an original short film that reflects this year's theme. Showcase your filmmaking skills in directing, cinematography, editing, and storytelling.

## Format
In-Person

## Submission Requirements

**Early Submission (2 weeks prior):**
- Unlisted YouTube/Vimeo link of short film
- Written statement (500 words max)
- Short Film Entry Form

**On-Site:**
- Three (3) copies of the written statement

## Registration Guidelines

1. A minimum of **one (1)** and a maximum of **six (6)** people can register.
2. **One submission per MIST team/school.**
3. More than 6 people may work on the project (acting, costume design, makeup, set design, etc.) but only up to 6 can be registered.

## Procedure

<Steps>

1. Submit **no more than one typed page** explanation of the film.

2. All work in the film must be completed by **registered competitors**, with exception of acting:
   - Filming
   - Directing
   - Scriptwriting
   - Editing

3. Actors may include others outside competitors, but **at least one team member** must be in the production.

4. Films may be **any genre**: fiction, documentary, animation, art, or experimental.

5. Film group allowed **3 minutes** to explain:
   - How they made the film
   - Purpose behind the film
   - What inspired them

6. All videos must be in **16:9 Aspect Ratio**.
   - Widescreen films must be letterboxed to fit 16:9.

7. Submit **unlisted YouTube link** to MyMIST **two (2) weeks prior** to competition.

8. Title and competitors' MIST ID must be **clearly identified** on submission.

9. Complete list of **crew members, cast, and equipment** must be submitted with short film.

10. **Short Film Entry Form** is required.
    - Points lost if not submitted.

11. Written statement **(500 words max)** must include:
    - What the short is about
    - What inspired you to make it
    - Answers to interview questions (Section C)

12. All films **MUST relate to this year's theme**.

13. Films may **NOT exceed 7 minutes** (excluding blank opening and closing credits).

14. Films must **NOT contain** inappropriate audio or video content.

15. If more than **15 entries**, only top 10 advance to interview.
    - Preliminary judging round before tournament weekend
    - Top 10 get 5-10 minute interview with Judges' Panel

16. Entries must be **original works** not previously distributed, awarded, or publicly exhibited.
    - Entrant must be creator with full copyright ownership

</Steps>

<Aside type="note" title="Important">
The written statement is an integral component of qualifying for an interview in the top ten.
</Aside>

## Interview Questions

Written statement should answer:
1. How did you aim to convey this year's theme in your work?
2. What do you hope to teach people about the theme with this work?
3. What element of the work is the most important towards conveying your understanding of the theme?

## Checklist

- [ ] Unlisted YouTube/Vimeo link (7 minutes max)
- [ ] Short Film Entry Form
- [ ] Written statement (500 words max) answering Section C questions
- [ ] Complete list of cast, crew, and equipment
- [ ] Submission labeled with MIST IDs and Competition Name
- [ ] 16:9 Aspect Ratio

## Short Film Entry Form

The entry form requires:
- MIST ID of group (or group name)
- Individual MIST IDs
- Number of people in group
- Film title
- Brief synopsis
- Film genre (Narrative Fiction, Documentary, Animation, Art, Experimental, Other)
- Length (minutes and seconds)
- Original format (Film, Video, Computer Graphic)
- Sound (Mono, Stereo, No sound/music)
- Production tools used (Camera, Hardware, Software, etc.)
- Owner of copyright
- Signature confirming rules understanding
