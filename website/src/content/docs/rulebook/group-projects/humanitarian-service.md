Design and execute a humanitarian service project that addresses a real need in your community while incorporating Islamic principles of charity and service.

**Format:** In-Person  
**Early Submission:** Project documentation (2 weeks prior)

:::note[Changed in 2026]
- Now partnered with HHRD instead of IRUSA
- Includes a new community engagement component to deepen local involvement and impact
:::

## Registration Guidelines

1. **Team of 3-5 students** from each school.
2. This is a **team competition**.

## Early Submission Requirements

Submit via MyMIST **two weeks before** the Regional Tournament:
- Humanitarian Service project documentation

## Project Requirements

Your project should include:

1. **Needs Assessment**
   - What community need did you identify?
   - How did you research this need?

2. **Project Planning**
   - Goals and objectives
   - Timeline and milestones
   - Resources needed

3. **Implementation**
   - What actions did your team take?
   - How many people did you reach/help?
   - Documentation (photos, testimonials, etc.)

4. **Community Engagement** *(New in 2026)*
   - How did you involve local community members?
   - Partnerships formed
   - Sustainable impact created

5. **Islamic Integration**
   - How does your project reflect Islamic values?
   - Connection to concepts like sadaqah, zakat, or community service

6. **Impact Measurement**
   - How did you measure success?
   - Quantitative and qualitative outcomes

7. **Reflection**
   - What did your team learn?
   - What would you do differently?

## HHRD Partnership

Projects may be coordinated with **Helping Hand for Relief and Development (HHRD)** for:
- Guidance on humanitarian best practices
- Potential partnership opportunities
- Resources and support

## On-Site Presentation

1. Teams present their project to judges.
2. Include documentation of your work.
3. Be prepared for Q&A from judges.
4. Visual aids strongly encouraged.

## Judging Criteria

- Significance of need addressed
- Quality of planning and execution
- Community engagement and involvement
- Measurable impact
- Islamic integration
- Sustainability and long-term thinking
- Presentation quality
- Team reflection and learning
