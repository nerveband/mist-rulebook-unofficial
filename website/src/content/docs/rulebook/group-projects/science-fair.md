Present a scientific research project or experiment that demonstrates inquiry, methodology, and connection to Islamic principles of seeking knowledge.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Format** | In-Person |
| **Type** | Team (1-3) |
| **Early Submission** | 2 weeks prior |

## Registration Guidelines

1. **Team of 1-3 students** from each school.
2. Students may compete individually or as a team.

## Early Submission Requirements

Submit via MyMIST **two weeks before** the Regional Tournament:
- Science Fair Abstract

## Abstract Requirements

Your abstract should include:
- Project title
- MIST ID (no names)
- Purpose/Question
- Hypothesis
- Methodology
- Results summary
- Conclusion

## Project Categories

Projects may fall into any scientific discipline:
- Biology
- Chemistry
- Physics
- Environmental Science
- Engineering
- Computer Science
- Mathematics
- Social Sciences

## Display Board Requirements

1. **Tri-fold display board** (standard science fair size)

2. Board should include:
   - Title
   - Question/Problem
   - Hypothesis
   - Materials and Methods
   - Data and Results
   - Analysis
   - Conclusion
   - References

3. **MIST ID only** - no names or school identifiers

4. Visual elements (graphs, photos, diagrams) encouraged

## On-Site Requirements

- Display board
- Any supplementary materials
- Lab notebook (if applicable)
- 3 hard copies of abstract

## Presentation

1. Teams present their project to judges.
2. Be prepared to explain methodology and findings.
3. Be ready for Q&A from judges.
4. Demonstrate understanding of scientific concepts.

## Judging Criteria

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Scientific Method** | Proper use of hypothesis, experimentation, analysis |
| **Research Quality** | Depth of investigation and data collection |
| **Analysis** | Interpretation of results and conclusions |
| **Display** | Organization and visual presentation |
| **Knowledge** | Understanding of concepts during Q&A |
| **Innovation** | Creativity and originality |

## Checklist
- [ ] Abstract submitted on MyMIST (2 weeks prior)
- [ ] Display board completed
- [ ] Lab notebook prepared (if applicable)
- [ ] 3 hard copies of abstract
- [ ] All team members prepared for presentation and Q&A
- [ ] MIST ID on all materials (no names)
