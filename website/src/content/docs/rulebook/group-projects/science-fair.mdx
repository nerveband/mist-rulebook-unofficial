Present original scientific research with active experiments, data analysis, and comprehensive findings in this team-based competition.

## Format
In-Person

## Submission Requirements

**Early Submission (2 weeks prior):**
- Abstract (5 pages or less) submitted to MyMIST
- Must include title page, abstract, bibliography, and citations

**On-Site:**
- Three (3) copies of the abstract
- Any physical visual aids
- Presentation (medium of choice)

## Registration Guidelines

1. A minimum of **one (1)** and a maximum of **six (6)** people can register.
2. **One submission per MIST team/school.**

## Procedure

<Steps>

1. Submit an **abstract (5 pages or less)** of your science fair project **two (2) weeks prior** to MyMIST.
   - Must include title page, abstract, bibliography, and citations.

2. Presentation must be **5-7 minutes minimum/maximum**.
   - **3 minutes** allocated for Q&A.

3. Science Fair projects may **NOT be used** if presented at previous MIST tournaments or other competitions.

4. **Visual aids are required** along with the speech.
   - If computer usage needed, bring aid on a USB.
   - Options: PowerPoint, posters, pictures, props, video clips
   - Encouraged to include figures, pictures, tables, and graphs.

5. Team must do their own **active research**:
   - Experiments, surveys, questionnaires, etc.
   - Results must be compiled and analyzed within the research paper.
   - If surveys/questionnaires conducted, must be included in presentation.

6. Present a **scientific research question** at the beginning of the abstract.
   - Question should be answered by the conclusion of the presentation.

7. At least **two sources** must be cited accurately within the abstract.
   - Works cited page required, separate from bibliography page.
   - Abstract and all citations must adhere to **MLA format**.
   - Abstract must be **maximum five (5) pages**.

8. Science Fair project should consist of **four components**:
   - Speech
   - Visual Aids
   - Bibliography (as part of abstract)
   - Abstract (with active research included)

9. Judging based on official MIST Science Fair ballot.

</Steps>

<Aside type="tip" title="Theme Bonus">
+3 bonus points can be awarded if the topic is relevant to the theme.
</Aside>

## Abstract Requirements

| Requirement | Specification |
|-------------|---------------|
| Length | 5 pages maximum |
| Font | 12-point Times New Roman |
| Spacing | Double-spaced |
| Margins | 1" on each side |
| Format | MLA style |
| Sources | At least 2 cited sources |

## Checklist

- [ ] Abstract (5 pages max) with title page, bibliography, citations
- [ ] Three (3) copies of abstract for judges
- [ ] Visual aids (poster board, PowerPoint, etc.)
- [ ] Active research data and analysis
- [ ] Works cited page (MLA format)
- [ ] Submission labeled with MIST IDs (all group members) and Competition Name
