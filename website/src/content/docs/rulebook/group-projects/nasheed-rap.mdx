Calling all aspiring singers! Write unique lyrics and use your voice to inspire others through original Islamic nasheed or rap performance.

## Format
In-Person

## Submission Requirements

**Early Submission:** None

**On-Site:**
- Three (3) copies of the Nasheed/Rap lyrics submitted to judges at time of competition

## Registration Guidelines

1. A minimum of **one (1)** and a maximum of **six (6)** people can register.
2. **One submission per MIST team/school.**
3. There will be **separate competitions** for brothers and sisters.
   - Each team must be either all sisters or all brothers.

## Procedure

<Steps>

1. Performance should **not exceed 5 minutes**.
   - Points deducted if performance exceeds allotted time.

2. Nasheed/Rap competition is **different from Spoken Word**.
   - If registered for both, you may NOT use the same piece.

3. The **only permitted instrument** is a drum with an open end (daf).
   - Full drum kits, floor drums, etc. are NOT allowed.
   - Drums will NOT be supplied by MIST - bring your own.
   - Vocal percussion (beatboxing) is permitted.
   - **No digitally enhanced sounds** for performance.

4. Groups must perform **in front of an audience**.

5. **No repeat performances** from previous years.
   - All nasheed/raps must be **original**.

6. Tones, lyrics, and tunes must **NOT be copied** from existing artists.
   - Copying is grounds for disqualification.

7. Submit **three hard copies** of Nasheed lyrics to judges **before performing**.

8. Judging based on official MIST Nasheed/Rap Ballot.

</Steps>

<Aside type="caution" title="Important">
All nasheed/raps must be original compositions. Copying from existing artists will result in disqualification.
</Aside>

## Interview Questions

Judges will ask:
1. How did you aim to convey this year's theme in your work?
2. What do you hope to teach people about the theme with your submission?
3. What element of the work is the most important towards conveying your understanding of the theme?

## Checklist

- [ ] Original nasheed/rap (not exceeding 5 minutes)
- [ ] Three (3) copies of lyrics
- [ ] Drum with open end (daf) if using percussion
- [ ] Submission labeled with MIST IDs and Competition Name
