Create an original short film that tells a compelling story related to the annual MIST theme.

**Format:** In-Person (screening)  
**Early Submission:** Film file (2 weeks prior)

## Registration Guidelines

1. **Team of 2-6 students** from each school.
2. This is a **team competition**.

## Early Submission Requirements

Submit via MyMIST **two weeks before** the Regional Tournament:
- Short film file (video format specified by Regional HQ)

## Film Requirements

1. **Length:** Check with Regional HQ for time limits (typically 5-10 minutes)

2. **Original work:** Film must be created by the team.

3. **Theme connection:** Must clearly relate to annual MIST theme.

4. **Appropriate content:** No inappropriate language, violence, or themes.

5. **Credits:** Include MIST ID only in credits (no names).

## Technical Specifications

- Video format: MP4 preferred (check with Regional HQ)
- Resolution: 720p minimum, 1080p recommended
- Audio: Clear and balanced

## Content Guidelines

Films should:
- Tell a coherent story
- Be appropriate for all audiences
- Avoid realistic depictions of animate beings where possible (consult Regional HQ)
- Include meaningful connection to theme

## Judging Criteria

| Category | Points |
|----------|--------|
| **Story/Script** | Quality of narrative and dialogue |
| **Theme Integration** | Connection to annual theme |
| **Cinematography** | Camera work, framing, visual style |
| **Editing** | Pacing, transitions, coherence |
| **Sound** | Audio quality, music, sound design |
| **Acting** | Performance quality |
| **Creativity** | Originality and innovation |
| **Overall Impact** | Entertainment value, memorability |

## On-Site Component

1. Films will be screened for judges.
2. Team may be asked to introduce their film.
3. Be prepared for Q&A about the filmmaking process.

## Tips for Success

1. **Plan thoroughly** - Storyboard before filming
2. **Good audio is crucial** - Poor sound quality hurts scores
3. **Light your scenes** - Proper lighting makes a big difference
4. **Edit carefully** - Pacing and timing matter
5. **Tell a complete story** - Beginning, middle, and end
