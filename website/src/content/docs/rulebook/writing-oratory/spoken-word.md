If you're more of the performer type of poet, then spoken word is for you! Here's your chance to convey your passion and originality, all while inspiring those around you.

## On-Site Requirements
- Three (3) copies of the Spoken Word piece must be submitted to judges on-site at competition time.

## Registration Guidelines

1. A limit of **two (2) students** from each team may register.
2. Each student competes individually; collaboration not permitted.

## Procedures

1. Competitors must come prepared with **3 copies** of their Spoken Word pieces.
   - MIST will not photocopy any pieces.
   - Compete with the piece submitted at that time.

2. Pieces must be labeled with **MIST ID ONLY**.

3. All competitors report to the competition room at designated time.

4. **No AI permitted** - usage is grounds for point loss and/or disqualification.

5. Pieces will be judged on: writing, performance, originality, sincerity, and inspiration.

6. The piece must be **relevant to the annual theme** for full marks.

7. **Performance should not exceed 6 minutes**.
   - Violation results in being ranked last when time signals given.
   - Judges must give time signals per competitor's request.

8. **No instruments** can be used.

## Scoring Rubric

| Section | Category | Points |
|---------|----------|--------|
| A | Technical | /10 |
| B | Content (Written) | /35 |
| C | Stage Presence | /25 |
| C | Familiarity | /20 |
| D | Overall Experience | /10 |
| **Total** | | **/100** |

### Section A: Technical (10 points)
- Piece lasted less than 6 minutes
- No identifiers aside from MIST ID
- Looks credible and dressed for interview
- Three (3) copies provided

### Section B: Content/Written (35 points)

:::caution
If piece contains profanity, sexual innuendo, or inappropriate content, score 0/35 and stop scoring this section.
:::

**Content:**
- Structure easy to follow
- Well written (rhyme, metaphors, figurative language present)
- Shows originality and creativity

### Section C: Performance

**Stage Presence (25 points)**
- Connection with audience, energy, excitement
- Makes use of stage space (movement, body language)
- Bodily movements used effectively
- Grabs attention, conveys sincerity and inspiration

**Familiarity (20 points)**
- Words clear and easy to understand
- Pronunciation acceptable
- Rate, pitch, volume appropriate
- Vocal variety adequate
- Sets adequate mood relating to piece
- **Poem memorized**

### Section D: Overall Experience (1-10 points)
Rate the experience:
- 1 = very poor
- 4-6 = average
- 10 = exceptional
