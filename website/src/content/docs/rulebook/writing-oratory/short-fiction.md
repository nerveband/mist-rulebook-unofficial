Competitors submit an original work of fiction no longer than 10 pages that relates to the theme and can be applied to a competitor-defined target audience. Illustrations are permitted for younger intended audiences.

## Registration Guidelines

1. A limit of **two (2) students** from each team may register.
2. Each student competes individually; collaboration not permitted.

## Procedures

1. Competitors must identify a **target audience** on the title page.

2. The piece must clearly relate to the theme but be applicable to a general audience.

3. **No AI permitted** - usage is grounds for point loss and/or disqualification.

4. Submit **PDF copy via MyMIST** two weeks before Regional Tournament.
   - Late submissions result in point deduction.

5. This is an **entirely virtual competition**.
   - Typed in black ink only.

6. **All submissions must:**
   - Be double-spaced
   - Have a cover page with title, MIST ID, and intended audience age group only
   - Be typed on 8.5 x 11 inch paper
   - Be **no more than 10 pages** (excluding title page and written statement)
   - Times New Roman, 12 pt font
   - Include a written statement based on interview questions

## Scoring Rubric

| Section | Category | Points |
|---------|----------|--------|
| A | Technical | /5 |
| B | Conventions | /5 |
| B | Ideas and Development | /15 |
| B | Organization | /10 |
| B | Sentence Fluency | /15 |
| C | Word Choice | /20 |
| C | Voice | /15 |
| D | Application of Theme | /15 |
| **Total** | | **/100** |

### Section A: Technical (5 points)
- Piece does not exceed 10 pages
- No identifiers aside from MIST ID

### Section B: Content

**Conventions (5 points)**
- Mostly free of spelling mistakes
- Mostly free of punctuation mistakes
- Mostly free of grammatical mistakes

**Ideas and Development (15 points)**
- Addressed theme clearly
- Unique approach to theme
- Stayed on topic throughout
- Main idea clear to reader

**Organization (10 points)**
- Title relevant and applicable
- Logical series of events
- Adequate details leading to natural flow (intro, plot, climax, resolution)
- Well-developed paragraphs with natural transitions
- Original story, not clich√©

**Sentence Fluency (15 points)**
- Varied sentence types
- Most sentences begin with different words
- Sentences flow well together
- Dialogue used where appropriate

### Section C: Style

**Word Choice (20 points)**
- Thoughtful adjectives create vivid imagery
- Strong action verbs
- Precise nouns
- Dialogue formatted appropriately
- Words create color and texture

**Voice (15 points)**
- Clear voice in the story
- Natural and consistent tone
- Captures mood with words
- Appropriately reaches target audience

### Section D: Application of Theme (15 points)
- Strong, clearly evident relevance to theme
- Theme expressed in unique way
- Purpose and inspiration clearly articulated
- Understandable for Muslim and non-Muslim alike
