Have you been inspired by this year's MIST theme? The prepared essay competition is perfect if you're looking for an outlet. This is your chance to be creative and persuade us on the significance of your chosen topic. Share the passion!

## Format
Virtual (Online)

## Early Submission Requirements

- Competitors must submit their Prepared Essay **2 weeks before** the Regional Tournament via MyMIST.

## Registration Guidelines

1. A limit of **two (2) students** from each team may register for this competition.
2. Each student competes **individually** — collaboration is not permitted.

## Procedure

<Steps>

1. Competitors shall write about any topic that reflects this year's **theme**.
   - The topic must refer to authentic sources of Islamic knowledge and historical facts.
   - Examples: stories from trusted scholars or reliable Islamic institutions (e.g., Yaqeen Institute).

2. The essay must be typed and **double-spaced** on white, 8.5 × 11 paper.

3. The essay **MUST have a cover page** with title and MIST ID only.
   - Competitor's name must not appear anywhere on the submission.

4. If outside sources are used, include proper **MLA citations** and a "Works Cited" page.

5. **No AI permitted** — usage is grounds for point loss and/or disqualification.

6. A **PDF copy** must be submitted via MyMIST 2 weeks before the Regional Tournament.
   - Late submissions result in point deduction.
   - Your region may have different deadlines — contact Regional Competitions HQ.

7. This is an **entirely virtual competition** — no copy required at tournament.
   - Entries should be typed in black ink only.

8. Essay length: minimum **1,500 words**, maximum **2,000 words**.
   - All abbreviations and articles (excluding a, an, the) will be counted.
   - Only the first 2,000 countable words will be read.

9. Competitors will be judged according to the Prepared Essay Ballot.

</Steps>

## Checklist

- [ ] PDF copy of essay on MyMIST (2 weeks prior)
- [ ] Written statement based on interview questions (Section D)
- [ ] Cover page with title and MIST ID only
- [ ] Works Cited page if using outside sources
- [ ] Labeled with MIST ID and competition name
