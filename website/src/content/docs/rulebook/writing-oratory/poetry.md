## Registration Guidelines

1. A limit of **two (2) students** from each team may register.
2. Each student competes individually; collaboration not permitted.

## Procedures

1. Poetry must clearly reflect upon any aspect of this year's theme.

2. Submissions must be labeled with **MIST ID and title only**.

3. Competitors may incorporate any style: rhymed, lyric, dramatic, narrative, or free verse.

4. **No AI permitted** - usage is grounds for point loss and/or disqualification.

5. All poems must have a title and **not exceed 40 lines** (excluding title).

6. Poems must be **original work** of the entrant.
   - Never published, self-published, or broadcast before January.

7. Submit a **PDF copy via MyMIST** two weeks before the Regional Tournament.
   - Late submissions result in point deduction.

8. This is an **entirely virtual competition** - no copies needed at tournament.

9. Entries should be typed in **black ink only**.

10. Entries can be double or single-spaced.

11. **Must include a filled-out "Poetry Analyzing Written Statement."**

## Poetry Analyzing Written Statement

Include the following sections:

### Section 1: Poem Identity
- Form of Poetry (Ballad, Sonnet, etc.)

### Section 2: Explanation of Poem
- Explanation in 250 words or less

### Section 3: Elements of Poetry
- Identify at least **five (5) elements** of poetry
- State line number, type of element, and explanation
- Examples: alliteration, metaphor, assonance, personification

### Section 4: Example of Inferences
- Provide an example of inference from your writing
- State line number and explain what you wished to infer

### Section 5: Mechanics, Grammar, Spelling (500 words or less)
1. In what ways does your piece represent your individuality?
2. Are there subtleties you hoped would come across without explanation?
3. Do you feel you conveyed the theme well? How?

## Scoring Rubric

| Section | Category | Points |
|---------|----------|--------|
| A | Technical | /10 |
| B | Poem Elements | /30 |
| C | Overall Impression | /25 |
| D | Written Statement Criteria | /25 |
| E | Overall Experience | /10 |
| **Total** | | **/100** |

### Section A: Technical (10 points)
- Poem does not exceed 40 lines
- Poetry Worksheet completed
- Typed in black ink only
- No identifiers aside from MIST ID

### Section B: Poem Elements (30 points)
:::caution
If piece contains inappropriate content, score 0/30 and stop scoring this section.
:::
- Structure easy to follow
- Form appropriate to subject
- Sensory details and figurative language create vivid images
- Sound devices (rhyme, alliteration, onomatopoeia) used effectively
- Word choice vivid and exact throughout

### Section C: Overall Impression (25 points)
- Title clearly stated
- Creates appropriate mood
- Conveys creativity and originality
- Grabs attention and conveys sincerity

### Section D: Written Statement Criteria (25 points)
- Section 1: Poem Identity
- Section 2: Coherent Explanation
- Section 3: Elements of Poetry
- Section 4: Example of Inference
- Section 5: Mechanics, Grammar, Spelling

### Section E: Overall Experience (1-10 points)
Rate the experience:
- 1 = very poor
- 4-6 = average
- 10 = exceptional
