Have you been inspired by this year's MIST theme? The prepared essay competition is perfect if you're looking for an outlet. This is your chance to be creative and persuade us on the significance of your chosen topic.

**Format:** Online (Virtual)  
**Early Submission:** 2 weeks before Regional Tournament

## Registration Guidelines

1. A limit of **two (2) students** from each team may register.
2. Each student competes individually; collaboration not permitted.

## Procedures

1. Write about any topic that reflects this year's theme.
   - Must refer to **authentic sources of Islamic knowledge** and historical facts.
   - Can include stories from trusted scholars or reliable Islamic institutions (e.g., Yaqeen Institute).

2. The essay must be **typed and double-spaced**.
   - On white, 8.5 x 11 paper.

3. **Cover page required** with title and MIST ID only.
   - Competitor name must NOT appear anywhere.

4. If outside sources are used, include **proper MLA citations** and a "Works Cited" page.

5. **No AI permitted** - usage is grounds for point loss and/or disqualification.

6. Submit **PDF copy via MyMIST** two weeks before Regional Tournament.
   - Late submissions result in point deduction.
   - Your region may have different deadlines - contact Regional HQ.

7. This is an **entirely virtual competition**.
   - Typed in black ink only.

8. Essay length: **minimum 1,500 words, maximum 2,000 words**.
   - All abbreviations and articles (excluding a, an, the) will be counted.
   - Only first 2,000 countable words will be read.

## Checklist
- [ ] PDF copy of essay on MyMIST (2 weeks prior)
- [ ] Written statement based on interview questions (Section D)
- [ ] Cover page with title and MIST ID only
- [ ] Works Cited page if sources used
- [ ] 1,500-2,000 words

## Scoring Rubric

| Section | Category | Points |
|---------|----------|--------|
| A | Conventions | /5 |
| A | Presentation | /5 |
| B | Ideas and Development | /25 |
| B | Organization | /25 |
| B | Sentence Fluency | /10 |
| C | Word Choice | /10 |
| C | Voice | /5 |
| D | Application of Theme | /15 |
| **Total** | | **/100** |

### Section A: Technical

**Conventions (5 points)**
- Mostly free of spelling mistakes
- Mostly free of grammatical mistakes
- Mostly free of punctuation mistakes

**Presentation (5 points)**
- Essay is typed
- Double spaced with size 12 font
- Cover page with title and MIST ID only
- Between 1,500-2,000 words
- Quotes properly credited

### Section B: Content

**Ideas and Development (25 points)**
- Addressed topic with sufficient detail
- Unique approach to topic
- Stayed on topic throughout
- Balance of showing and telling
- Theme/message clear to reader
- Outside information used and cited

**Organization (25 points)**
- Introduction with unique start, clear thesis, concise controls
- Adequate details and support for each control
- Title stands for entire essay
- Conclusion links back to introduction

**Sentence Fluency (10 points)**
- Varied sentence types
- Most sentences begin with different words
- Rhythm when read aloud
- Logical progression of ideas

### Section C: Style

**Word Choice (10 points)**
- Thoughtful adjectives create imagery
- Strong action verbs
- Precise nouns
- Words create color and texture

**Voice (5 points)**
- Shows passion about topic
- Natural and consistent tone
- Captures mood with words

### Section D: Application of Theme (15 points)
- Strong, clearly evident relevance to theme
- Theme expressed in unique way
- Purpose and inspiration clearly articulated
- Essay understandable for Muslim and non-Muslim alike
