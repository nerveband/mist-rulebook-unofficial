If you're more of the performer type of poet, then spoken word is for you! Here's your chance to convey your passion and originality, all while inspiring those around you.

## Format
In-Person

## On-Site Submission Requirements

- Three (3) copies of the Spoken Word piece must be submitted to judges on-site at the time of the competition.

## Registration Guidelines

1. A limit of **two (2) students** from each team may register for this competition.
2. Each student competes **individually** — collaboration is not permitted.

## Procedure

<Steps>

1. Competitors must come prepared with copies of their Spoken Word Pieces.
   - MIST will NOT photocopy any spoken word pieces.
   - Competitor must compete with the piece submitted at that time.

2. Pieces must be labeled with **MIST ID ONLY**. Competitor's name must not appear anywhere on the work.

3. All competitors report to the competition room at the same time (designated in competition schedule).

4. **No AI permitted** — usage is grounds for point loss and/or disqualification.

5. Spoken Word pieces will be judged on: **writing, performance, originality, sincerity, and inspiration**.

6. The piece must be relevant to the **annual theme** to receive full marks in Section D.

7. Performance should **not exceed 6 minutes**.
   - Violation results in being ranked last when visible time signals have been given.
   - Judges/moderators are required to give time signals per competitor's request.

8. **No instruments** can be used for the performance.

</Steps>
