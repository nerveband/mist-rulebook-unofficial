Do you get an adrenaline rush at knowing the answer to any question asked? Are you a memorization whiz? Then MIST Quiz Bowl is the competition for you!

**Format:** In-Person (Bracket Competition)

:::note[Changed in 2026]
- Round 1 now focuses exclusively on current events with toss-up questions in a revised structure
- Rounds 2 and 3 have point adjustments for better scoring balance
- Total topics reduced from six to five
:::

## Registration Guidelines

1. A maximum of **four (4) students** from each team may register.
2. This is a **team competition**.

## Competition Format

MIST Quiz Bowl is a **Bracket Competition** with elimination rounds.

## Round Structure

### Round 1: Current Events
- Toss-up questions only
- Revised structure for clearer pacing

### Rounds 2 & 3: Topic-Based
- Mix of toss-up and bonus questions
- Adjusted point values for better balance

## Question Types

### Toss-Up Questions
- Individual buzzer questions
- First team to buzz with correct answer gets points
- Incorrect answers may result in point deduction or opponent opportunity

### Bonus Questions
- Awarded to team that correctly answers toss-up
- Team may confer before answering
- Multiple parts with separate point values

## Procedures

1. All team members report to competition room at designated time.

2. Teams sit at designated tables with buzzers.

3. **No electronic devices** during competition.

4. Moderator reads questions; teams buzz in to answer.

5. For toss-ups: Individual player answers without conferring.

6. For bonuses: Team may confer quietly before captain gives answer.

7. Challenges to questions must be made immediately after the question.

## Scoring

- Toss-up correct: +10 points (typical)
- Toss-up incorrect: -5 points or pass to opponent (varies by round)
- Bonus questions: Points vary by difficulty

## What to Study

All information in the topic materials is fair game, including:
- Graph/figure content (labels, numbers, statistics)
- Dates
- Numbers and statistics
- Names and backgrounds of speakers/individuals mentioned
- Definitions of key terms

:::note
References in source materials do not need to be memorized.
:::

## Audience Guidelines

Parents, coaches, and supporters are welcome to attend MIST Quiz Bowl. Please:
- Check with your Regional HQ to confirm
- Follow all audience guidelines (no communication with competitors)
- Remain silent during questions
