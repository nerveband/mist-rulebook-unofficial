This competition is a fun way to display your creativity under spontaneous and timed circumstances. Each team must impress the judges in various hypothetical scenarios while under pressure to think on their feet and make the audience laugh!

**Format:** In-Person (Bracket Competition)  
**Gender Segregated:** Yes (Brothers and Sisters compete separately)

## Registration Guidelines

1. A maximum of **four (4) students** from each team may register.
2. This is a **team competition**.
3. Competition is **gender segregated**:
   - Brothers Improv
   - Sisters Improv

## Competition Format

Improv is a **Bracket Competition** with elimination rounds.

### Rounds
- Preliminary Rounds
- Quarterfinals
- Semifinals
- Finals

## Procedures

1. All team members report to competition room at designated time.

2. Teams will be given a scenario or prompt to act out.

3. Teams have limited time to prepare (if any) before performing.

4. Performances are judged on creativity, humor, teamwork, and execution.

5. **No pre-written scripts or prepared material** - everything must be improvised.

6. **No inappropriate content** - keep performances clean and respectful.

7. All team members should participate actively.

## Types of Improv Games

Teams may be asked to perform various improv games including:
- Scene-based scenarios
- Character-driven scenes
- Word association games
- Physical comedy challenges
- Musical improv (without instruments)

## Judging Criteria

| Criteria | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Creativity** | Originality of ideas and approaches |
| **Teamwork** | How well team members work together |
| **Humor** | Entertainment value and comedic timing |
| **Execution** | Quality of performance and commitment |
| **Quick Thinking** | Ability to think on feet and adapt |

## Rules

1. **No props** unless provided by organizers.

2. **No physical contact** that could be considered inappropriate.

3. **Time limits** must be respected - performances will be stopped if overtime.

4. **All team members must participate** in each round.

5. Maintain **good sportsmanship** - support other teams.

## Tips for Success

1. **Say "Yes, and..."** - Accept what your teammates offer and build on it
2. **Listen actively** - Pay attention to what others are doing
3. **Make your partners look good** - Improv is about teamwork
4. **Commit fully** - Don't hold back in your performance
5. **Have fun** - The audience can tell when you're enjoying yourself

## Spectator Guidelines

Students who spectate must abide by **gender segregation guidelines**:
- Brothers are not permitted to spectate Sisters Improv
- Sisters are not permitted to spectate Brothers Improv
