Test your recitation of the Qur'an in Arabic according to Tajweed (rules of recitation). Unlike Memorization, this competition focuses on proper recitation and Tajweed rules.

## Format
In-Person

## Registration Guidelines

1. This is a **gender-segregated** competition.
   - Brothers' Qur'an Recitation
   - Sisters' Qur'an Recitation

2. Each team may have a maximum of **two (2) brothers** and **two (2) sisters**.

## Procedure

<Steps>

1. There are **no levels** for this competition - the **entirety of the Qur'an** is testable material.

2. Judges will tell the competitor **where to start** and **where to stop** the recitation.

3. Judging based on **three separate passages** from any part of the Qur'an.

4. The focus is **Qur'an recitation and Tajweed**, NOT memorization.

5. Competitors will recite **3 passages live** during their interview.

6. Each passage will be about **10 verses long**.

7. Students must bring their **own Qur'an** for the competition.
   - **Annotations NOT permitted**: highlighting, underlining, post-its, writing in margins
   - **Color-coded Qur'ans with Tajweed rules listed are NOT permitted**

8. The interview will include:
   - Recitation of up to 3 passages
   - Scoring on pronunciation (Tajweed) during recitation
   - Testing on **basic Tajweed rules** for final 10 points
   - Expectation to identify rules (place of articulation, qualities of letters, nasal sounds, types of madd, etc.)

9. Competitors will **NOT** be expected to have extensive knowledge on the laws of Tajweed.

</Steps>

<Aside type="caution" title="Qur'an Requirements">
Color-coded Qur'ans with Tajweed rules listed are NOT permitted. Bring a clean copy without annotations.
</Aside>

## Mistake Categories

### Minor Mistakes
| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Heavy vs. Light | Overlooking rules of tafkheem and tarqeeq when reciting Allah's name and letter Raa |
| Ghunnah | Not adhering to rules of izhaar, idghaam, iqlaab, ikhfaa in their respective places |
| Incorrect Makhraj or Sifat | Not pronouncing letters from correct place of articulation and respective qualities (qalqala, etc.) |
| Prolongation | Not prolonging/stretching a letter when madd is present |

### Major Mistakes
| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Addition | Adding letters to words or adding entire words |
| Omission | Leaving off letters from words or leaving out entire words |
| Replacement | Replacing a letter for another or replacing entire words |
| Change Harakah | Changing fat'ha, dhamma, or kasra |
| Harakah/Sukoon | Replacing harakah with sukoon or vice versa |

## What to Bring

- [ ] Personal Qur'an (no annotations or color-coding)
