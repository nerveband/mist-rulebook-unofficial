Read one of four books and take a test on it. Tests are comprised of multiple choice questions to judge reading comprehension and analytical abilities.

## Format
In-Person

## Number of Tests
4 (one per book)

<Aside type="tip" title="Book List">
Knowledge Test books are available on the MIST National's website: [www.getmistified.com/rulebook](https://www.getmistified.com/rulebook)
</Aside>

<Aside type="note" title="Equal Difficulty">
All levels will be of equal difficulty. Each test is on a different book, but every test will be formatted the same.
</Aside>

## Registration Guidelines

1. No more than **four (4) students** from each team may register for each test.
2. Each student may pick **only one test** to compete in.
   - Example: 4 students may register for Test 1, 4 other students for Test 2, etc.

## Procedure

<Steps>

1. Competitors have **1 hour and 30 minutes** to complete their test.

2. Competitors must achieve a score of **65% or above** in order to rank/win a medal.

3. Test contains **ONLY multiple choice** and multiple-multiple choice questions (select all that apply).
   - Questions will have an **analytical/thinking focus**.
   - Each test has an **essay tie-breaker bonus question** worth 10 points.

4. Tests are **open-book** and must be taken **individually**.
   - **Electronic devices NOT permitted** during competition.
   - **Electronic versions of books NOT permitted**.

5. **Annotations NOT permitted** within the book:
   - No highlighting
   - No underlining
   - No post-its
   - No writing in margins
   - All books will be checked before exam starts

6. Students must have their **own book** - sharing NOT allowed once testing begins.

7. **Printouts/photocopies** of the book are permitted but must be free of any notes.

8. All questions based on material covered in the **assigned book**.

9. **No ballot** for this competition - scores based solely on test performance.

</Steps>

<Aside type="caution" title="Important">
Annotations within the book are NOT permitted and all books will be checked before the exam starts. Printouts/photocopies must be free of any notes.
</Aside>

## Test Format

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Duration | 1 hour 30 minutes |
| Question Types | Multiple choice, Multiple-multiple choice (select all that apply) |
| Focus | Analytical and reading comprehension |
| Tie-Breaker | Essay bonus question (10 points) |
| Required Score to Place | 65% or above |
| Open Book | Yes (physical copy only) |
| Electronic Devices | Not permitted |

## What to Bring

- [ ] Physical copy of assigned book (no annotations)
- [ ] OR Clean printout/photocopy of book (no notes)
- [ ] Writing utensils
- [ ] MIST ID

## Scoring

- No official ballot - scores based entirely on test performance
- Must score **65% or above** to rank or win a medal
- Tie-breaker essay question worth 10 points for tied scores
