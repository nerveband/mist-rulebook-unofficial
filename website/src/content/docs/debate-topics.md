## 2026 Debate Topics

:::note
Topics may change for Nationals. Check with your Regional HQ for the most current resolutions.
:::

### Resolutions

1. **THS adopt a wealth tax.**

2. **THBT the United Nations should abolish permanent membership on its Security Council.**

3. **THBT the benefits of the use of generative artificial intelligence in education outweigh the harms.**

4. **THS substantially increase its investment in nuclear energy.**

5. **THBT immigration should be recognized as a human right.**

## Preparation Tips

1. **Research both sides** - You may be assigned either Government or Opposition
2. **Gather evidence** - Statistics, expert opinions, case studies
3. **Prepare frameworks** - How will you structure your arguments?
4. **Practice rebuttals** - Anticipate opposing arguments
5. **Know current events** - Topics often relate to real-world issues
